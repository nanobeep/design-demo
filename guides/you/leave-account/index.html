Basecamp Help > You > Leave an account

Leave an account and decline an invitation

Done working on a particular account? Basecamp lets you remove yourself.

Leaving an account

  1. Head to your Me page, and click "My Basecamp Settings".
  2. The option to leave an account is at the bottom.

Sorry, you can't remove yourself from individual projects!

Declining an invitation

  1. Just use the link in your invitation email. The account owner will get a message saying that you declined the invitation.

If you're already a member of a particular account, you can't decline invitations to new projects on that account.
