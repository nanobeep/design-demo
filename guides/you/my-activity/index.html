Your activity and assignments

Want to see what a busy little beaver you've been? Forget which project you just uploaded that file to? Your latest activity across all your projects is always accessible on your "Me" page!

  1. To check out your latest activity, head to your "Me" page and click "See all of your activity" for everything going back to the beginning of Basecamp time.

You can also see someone else's activity and assignments by going to the Everyone page and clicking on their avatar.

  1. View the tasks that have been assigned to you, and sort by due date. You can also view your completed assignments and the files you've uploaded. My, how productive you are!

Learn how to update your password and email settings on your "Me" page.
