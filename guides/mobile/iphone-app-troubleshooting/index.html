Older App Version

The latest Basecamp app requires iOS7. But don't worry if you've got iOS6, you can get an older version of the app. Instead of using your iOS device to get the app, open up iTunes on your computer and "buy" the app there. A "download last compatible version" screen will appear when you go back to the iPhone/iPod touch and try to grab the app.

Blank screen?

If you've got an iOS 7 device and upgrade to the new version (1.4.0) of the App, you might see blank pages when you first open it. Force quitting the app one time will fix the problem. When your app is open:

  1. Double-tap the home button on your iPhone.
  2. Slide the Basecamp app upwards off the screen.
  3. Re-open the Basecamp app and log in again.

We should have a fix for this soon!
