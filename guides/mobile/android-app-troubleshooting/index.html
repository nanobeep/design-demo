Android App Troubleshooting

Supported Devices

The latest Android app requires Android version 4.0.

Links in Notification Emails

Links in notification emails only open the Basecamp app when you have the account in question open. That means if you're working in your app in one account and try to open a notification link for another account, that link will open in Chrome.

We should have a fix for this soon!

Other problems?

Seeing other issues? Send our support team a ticket with a screenshot of your phone.
