Create a shortcut

If you use Basecamp from your mobile device, you can add a shortcut to quickly get access.

Android Devices

  1. Open your account in Google Chrome.
  2. Tap on the star and add it to your bookmarks.
  3. Use the menu button an open your bookmarks.
  4. Find your bookmark for Basecamp, press down and hold. An action menu will appear.
  5. Select "Add to home screen."

Windows Phone

  1. Open your account in Internet Explorer.
  2. Tap the three dots in the bottom right corner of the Windows Phone 7 and drag it up to view the options menu.
  3. Select the "pin to start" option to add the webpage to your Home Screen.

iPhone/iPad

  1. Open your account in Mobile Safari.
  2. Tap the arrow button, at the bottom of your screen or next to your address bar.
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" and name the bookmark "Basecamp."
