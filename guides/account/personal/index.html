Basecamp Personal

Basecamp Personal is a great way to use Basecamp for your smaller projects. Plan a vacation, track a home remodel, coordinate your volunteer activities ... anything at all!

Basecamp Personal is one single project. You're able to coordinate with five other account members. You get 1 GB of storage space. It's yours for a one-time payment of $25. It's currently only available to current Basecamp users (or anyone with a 37signals ID). We plan to open Basecamp Personal up to the wider public soon!

Since Basecamp Personal is limited to one project, it doesn't have the features that span multiple projects. You won't have a calendar, but you do have a page for project dates. There isn't a Progress feed, but you have a Catch Up page to keep you current. You can invite five additional people to join your account, but you won't be able to designate any of them as admins.
