Switching from Basecamp Classic

You're welcome to use your Basecamp Classic account as long as you like. When you're ready, it's easy for you to move your Classic projects into the new and improved version of Basecamp. And if you keep your Classic account open, the new Basecamp is yours for free!

Only the Basecamp Classic account owner can perform these steps.

Creating a new account

  1. Click the Account tab in Basecamp Classic. Scroll to the bottom of the page to set up your new Basecamp account.

  2. Click "Create my new Basecamp account," and bam! The Basecamp Classic account and the new Basecamp account will be linked, and you can start migrating projects.

  3. If you set up an account without going through Basecamp Classic, don't worry! You can link them together on your Account page in the new Basecamp.

    To link accounts, you'll need to own both of them, under the same ID.

Migrate your projects

Your Account page in the new Basecamp will give you the option to migrate your projects over from Basecamp Classic. Click 'Choose which projects to move'.

  1. Pick a project to migrate: You can migrate any project in your Classic account.

    When you migrate a project, it will automatically be archived in Basecamp Classic.

Changes from Classic

Classic projects migrate to the new Basecamp very easily, but there are a few changes you may want to keep in mind:

  • The new Basecamp doesn't currently have time tracking. If that is crucial to your use of Basecamp, you might want to stick with Basecamp Classic.
  • Writeboards will migrate as Text Documents. Earlier drafts of Writeboards won't migrate, nor will comments on Writeboards.
  • File and message categories are not migrated.
  • Milestones will become project Events.

Unlinking your account

If you don't want your Classic and new accounts linked anymore, you can unlink them from the Account page in new Basecamp.

  1. From your account page, scroll down to Move projects from Basecamp Classic and click "Unlink this Basecamp Classic account."
