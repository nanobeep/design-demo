Passwords and invitations

Resetting passwords

  1. You can send a password reset email to any member of your account.

  2. After an unsuccessful login attempt, folks will see the option to have a password reset sent to them.

    Only the individual user can change their own email address (from the Me page), since each user owns their own 37signals ID, which can be used across multiple accounts.

Resending an invitation to someone

  1. To resend an invitation, head to "Everyone" ...
  2. Find and click on that person …
  3. Finally, click "re-send the email" to send them a reminder. You can also copy the RSVP URL and pass that along to your invitee.

    If you're not seeing this, that person has already accepted your invite!
