To-do lists and tasks

Make a new list

  1. Click "Add a to-do list"

  2. Give the list a name, and start adding tasks.

  3. You can assign the task to someone and add a due date. Dated to-dos will appear on your calendar.

    When you assign a task to another user, the assignee will receive an email notification. You won't get the initial notification for to-dos you assign to yourself. All users will get a notification the day before the task is due. You'll get a notification when the task is completed by another user, unless you opt out.

  4. Reorder your tasks by clicking on the task and dragging. You can move tasks to new lists, and reorder entire lists that way too.

    Reordering your lists is a great way to set priorities. First on the list? Highest priority! Watch a video on how to organize and prioritize your to-dos.

  5. Comments You can add comments to your individual tasks, and to the entire list. Just click on the comment or list title.

  6. Basecamp doesn't have sublists, but here's a tip! Create your own outline by adding numbers to your main tasks, and asterisks to the subtasks. Click and drag to order your lists the way you need them.
