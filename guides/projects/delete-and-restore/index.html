Deleting and restoring project items

Deleting

  1. Click to view an item: If you're an admin, you'll see the option to delete or edit a post. Non-admins can only delete or edit items they've created, and only for 15 minutes.

Deleting files

Delete files to free up space in your account! To delete a file, just click on the file so that it's enlarged. From there, click the "Delete..." link on the right.

Only admins can edit or delete items they did not create.

Email signature attachments will be ignored as long as they are GIF, JPG, smaller than 3KB, and follow the "image00#" filename format. Images beginning with "~WRD" or "compose-unknown-contact" are also ignored.

To-do lists and tasks

  1. Hover over the title of a list or a task. You'll see a trash can icon pop up to the left. Click on that that to delete.

Any member of a project can delete a to-do list or task.

Restore deleted items, or delete them forever

Don't panic if you delete something! You can restore deleted items for up to 30 days.

  1. To see all deleted items, find the Trash can on your Everything page.
  2. Here, you can see the history of all deleted items you had access to. Click on an individual item to restore it or delete it permanently.
  3. Individual comments can be restored right from the item's history.

    Anyone with access to a project can delete to-do lists and tasks. Admins can restore those items. Other projects items can only be deleted and restored by admins.
